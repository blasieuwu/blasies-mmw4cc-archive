# blasieuwu's simple guide on charting

hello, i'm **@blasieuwu**, and here's how you can make **custom pjsk charts** and how you can play them on untitledcharts.com!

## what you need:

- a pc
- a charting software
  - i'd suggest using **[MMW4UC](https://github.com/UntitledCharts/MikuMikuWorld4UC/releases/tag/v3.2.1.37)**
  - if you want to use old software, try **[MMW4CC or MMW](https://github.com/blasieuwu/blasies-mmw4cc-archive/releases/tag/archive)**
- the **song you want to chart** in .mp3 (it can be **a section of the song** or **the full song**)
- the song cover image (.png/.jpg)

## step 1: making the chart
1. create a dedicated folder on your pc to hold your working assets (e.g., `bgm.mp3` and `cover.png`).
2. open up your **charting software** of choice.
3. load in the **song** and the **"jacket"** (the song cover).
4. get to charting!

> 💡 **pro-tip for asset updates:** if you need to swap the audio or cover image later while working, use the windows **"Save As"** menu to save the new file directly over the old one in your project directory. hitting "yes" to overwrite the existing file instantly forces windows to swap the data bytes while keeping your exact configuration paths perfect without breaking the project.

another useful tip:
- try placing **notes at the beats first** before making the chart itself. it's a good way to **keep it timed right at the beginning!**

once you're done, **export the chart!** depending on your tool:
- **MMW4UC:** export as **"Sonolus Level Data"** (.json.gz)
- **MMW4CC:** export as **.usc (Universal Sekai Chart)**
- **MMW:** export as **.sus (Sliding Universal Score)**

## step 2: publishing the chart

1. go to [untitledcharts.com](https://www.untitledcharts.com/)
2. sign in with your **Sonolus** account.
3. go to your Dashboard.
4. click **"+ New Chart"**.
5. fill in the details:
  - Song Title
  - Artist(s)
  - Charter Name
  - Level (aka. difficulty — note that negative numbers like `-1` work natively if you're making joke charts!)
  - Description (optional)
  - Tags
6. upload your **cover image (.png/.jpg), audio (.mp3), and chart file (.json.gz, .usc, or .sus)**.
7. optionally, you can add a **preview audio (.mp3)** and a **background image (.png/.jpg)**.
8. change the visibility toggle to your preference.
9. hit **"Upload Chart"**.

you're done!

## step 3: playing the chart

1. open Sonolus on your phone.
2. go to the **UntitledCharts** server (add `untitledcharts.com` to your server list if you haven't already).
3. sign in with your account.
4. enter the **song name** you made for the chart into the search bar.
5. boot up the track and play!

and that's it! this is the **pure essential guide to charting** on UntitledCharts.
